About Rajalaut Bunaken

Born From the Sea,
Guided by Passion

Rajalaut Bunaken is a locally-owned dive and tour operator rooted in the stunning waters of Bunaken National Marine Park — one of the world's richest marine biodiversity hotspots. We craft unforgettable experiences for every level of diver and traveler.

From adrenaline-fueled wall dives and encounters with turtles and napoleon wrasse, to serene island-hopping and cultural village tours — we bring the real Bunaken to life.

🏅
PADI Certified
All instructors are internationally certified with safety-first protocols.
🌊
Local Expertise
Born & raised on Bunaken — we know every reef, current & creature.
🐢
Eco-Conscious
We protect the reef through responsible tourism and coral restoration.
🛟
Safety First
Modern equipment, O2 kits onboard, and strict depth protocols.
